CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE FOR THIS TEMPLATE
=====================================

For each application, customize these elements:

1. RESEARCH COMPANY THOROUGHLY:
   - Review last 3-6 months of company blog posts, press releases, LinkedIn updates
   - Identify recent strategic initiatives, product launches, or market expansions
   - Note any quotes from executives about strategic priorities
   - Find mutual connections on LinkedIn who can provide insider perspective

2. TAILOR "WHAT DISTINGUISHES MY CANDIDACY" SECTION:
   - Reorder the four bullet points based on what the job description emphasizes most
   - If role is technical-heavy: Lead with Analytical Rigor
   - If role is strategy/consulting: Lead with Proven Business Impact
   - If role is marketing operations: Lead with Marketing Foundation

3. CUSTOMIZE "PARTICULARLY DRAWN TO" PARAGRAPH:
   - Tier 1 Priority (Red Ventures, HubSpot, Salesforce): Write custom 3-4 sentences showing deep company knowledge
   - Tier 2 Companies: Write 2-3 sentences connecting role to your interests
   - Include specific reference to recent company news, product, or initiative

4. INTEGRATE KEYWORDS FROM JOB DESCRIPTION:
   - Identify 5-7 critical technical skills or phrases in job posting
   - Weave these naturally into your experience descriptions
   - Use exact terminology they use (e.g., if they say "campaign attribution" don't say "marketing ROI tracking")

5. QUANTIFY EVERYTHING:
   - Replace vague claims with specific numbers whenever possible
   - Examples: "Led team of 5" vs. "Led team"
   - "Analyzed 170+ survey responses" vs. "Analyzed survey data"
   - "40% workflow efficiency improvement" vs. "Improved workflows"

6. DEMONSTRATE KNOWLEDGE OF THEIR BUSINESS MODEL:
   - For SaaS companies: Reference ARR growth, expansion revenue, product-led growth
   - For agencies: Mention client retention, campaign performance, creative excellence
   - For corporate: Discuss digital transformation, operational efficiency, strategic positioning
