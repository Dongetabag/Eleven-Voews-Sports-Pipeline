# Simeon Views Tour Team Structure & Compensation Report

*Comprehensive analysis of team requirements, roles, and compensation for a profitable 10-city hip-hop tour based on funding projections and industry standards*

Based on Simeon Views' profile: Collaborative artist influenced by Drake, Kendrick, and Meek Mill; emphasis on team leadership from "I Called It"; targeting $35K-$75K tour funding.

---

## Executive Summary

**Tour Funding Scenarios:**
- **Low-End Budget**: $35,000 (Basic team, essential roles only)
- **High-End Budget**: $75,000 (Premium team, full professional support)
- **Tour Revenue Projection**: $85,000-$150,000 gross
- **Profit Margins**: 35-55% after all expenses

**Team Philosophy**: Following Simeon Views' "I Called It" ethos of collaborative leadership, this structure emphasizes shared responsibility, performance bonuses, and long-term relationship building.

## Low-End Team Structure ($35,000 Budget)

*Essential roles for basic tour execution with 15-20% profit margin*

### Core Team (6 People)

#### 1. Tour Manager
**Role**: Overall coordination, logistics, day-to-day operations
**Responsibilities:**
- Daily schedule management and venue coordination
- Transportation and accommodation logistics
- Emergency problem solving and crisis management
- Liaison between artist and venues/sponsors
- Budget tracking and expense management

**Compensation Structure:**
- **Base Fee**: $3,500 for full tour (10 cities)
- **Per Diem**: $75/day × 25 days = $1,875
- **Performance Bonus**: $500 if tour meets revenue targets
- **Total**: $5,875

**Time Commitment:**
- Pre-tour: 40 hours (planning, venue communication)
- Tour: 25 days full-time
- Post-tour: 10 hours (wrap-up, reporting)

**Ideal Profile**: 3+ years music industry experience, strong organizational skills, crisis management ability

#### 2. Sound Engineer/FOH
**Role**: Live sound mixing, equipment management
**Responsibilities:**
- Front-of-house sound mixing during performances
- Sound check supervision and equipment setup
- Coordination with venue sound systems
- Basic equipment troubleshooting
- Ensuring optimal audio quality for sponsors recording content

**Compensation Structure:**
- **Base Fee**: $350 per show × 10 shows = $3,500
- **Setup/Teardown**: $100 per show × 10 = $1,000
- **Travel Days**: $150 × 5 travel days = $750
- **Total**: $5,250

**Time Commitment:**
- Setup: 4 hours per venue
- Sound check: 2 hours per venue
- Show: 3 hours per venue
- Teardown: 2 hours per venue
- Total: 110 hours over tour

**Ideal Profile**: Audio engineering degree or equivalent experience, hip-hop mixing experience, portable equipment knowledge

#### 3. Videographer/Content Creator
**Role**: Tour documentation, social media content, sponsor integration
**Responsibilities:**
- Daily video content creation for social media
- Performance footage for promotional use
- Behind-the-scenes documentation
- Sponsor activation content creation
- Basic video editing and immediate posting

**Compensation Structure:**
- **Base Fee**: $2,500 for full tour
- **Content Creation Bonus**: $50 per posted video (target: 50 videos)
- **Sponsor Content**: $200 per sponsor integration piece
- **Total**: $5,000-$6,500 (depending on content output)

**Time Commitment:**
- Filming: 4-6 hours per day on tour
- Editing: 2-3 hours per day
- Social media management: 1 hour per day
- Total: 175-225 hours over tour

**Ideal Profile**: Social media savvy, hip-hop culture understanding, efficient video editing skills

#### 4. Merchandise Manager
**Role**: Merch sales, inventory management, sponsor material coordination
**Responsibilities:**
- Merchandise setup and sales at venues
- Inventory tracking and ordering
- Sponsor material distribution
- Meet-and-greet coordination
- Daily sales reporting

**Compensation Structure:**
- **Base Fee**: $200 per show × 10 shows = $2,000
- **Commission**: 5% of merchandise sales (projected $10K = $500)
- **Sponsor Material Handling**: $300 total
- **Total**: $2,800

**Time Commitment:**
- Setup: 2 hours per venue
- Sales during show: 4 hours per venue
- Inventory management: 1 hour per day
- Total: 85 hours over tour

**Ideal Profile**: Sales experience, organized, comfortable with fan interaction

#### 5. Social Media Manager/Assistant
**Role**: Real-time social media, fan engagement, sponsor coordination
**Responsibilities:**
- Live social media posting during shows
- Fan engagement and response management
- Sponsor social media coordination
- Basic graphic design for posts
- Analytics tracking and reporting

**Compensation Structure:**
- **Base Fee**: $2,000 for full tour
- **Engagement Bonus**: $200 if social media targets met
- **Sponsor Coordination**: $500 total
- **Total**: $2,700

**Time Commitment:**
- Daily posting: 2-3 hours per day
- Engagement: 1-2 hours per day
- Analytics and reporting: 5 hours per week
- Total: 125 hours over tour

**Ideal Profile**: Social media expertise, hip-hop culture knowledge, graphic design basics

#### 6. Driver/Security
**Role**: Transportation, basic security, equipment transport
**Responsibilities:**
- Safe transportation between cities
- Basic crowd management and artist protection
- Equipment loading and unloading
- Emergency assistance and support

**Compensation Structure:**
- **Base Fee**: $200 per day × 25 days = $5,000
- **Fuel and vehicle expenses**: $2,500
- **Accommodation sharing**: Included in tour budget
- **Total**: $7,500

**Time Commitment:**
- Driving: 8-12 hours on travel days
- Setup assistance: 2 hours per venue
- Security during shows: 4 hours per venue
- Total: 150+ hours over tour

**Ideal Profile**: Clean driving record, basic security training, physical capability for equipment handling

- Emergency fund and miscellaneous expenses
- Additional equipment rental if needed
